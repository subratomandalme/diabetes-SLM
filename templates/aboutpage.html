About DiabetesGPT

Learn about our mission to transform diabetes management through accessible AI assistance and expert guidance.

Our Mission

DiabetesGPT was created with a simple yet powerful mission: to make diabetes management knowledge accessible to everyone. We believe that personalized guidance shouldn't be limited by geography, financial resources, or healthcare access.

By leveraging advanced artificial intelligence, we've built a platform that provides instant, evidence-based advice tailored to individual needs. Our goal is to empower people living with diabetes to make informed decisions about their health every day.

How We Started

DiabetesGPT began as a passion project in 2024, when our founder, a healthcare technologist living with Type 1 diabetes, recognized the potential for AI to provide personalized diabetes guidance at scale.

Working with a team of endocrinologists, certified diabetes educators, and AI specialists, we developed a specialized language model trained on the latest diabetes management research, clinical guidelines, and practical advice that people with diabetes need in their daily lives.

Our Technology

DiabetesGPT is powered by a custom large language model (LLM) that has been specifically trained on diabetes management literature, medical journals, nutritional data, and exercise science. This focused training allows our AI to provide relevant, accurate information tailored to diabetes care.

Our system is continuously updated with the latest research and guidelines to ensure the advice provided remains current. While we strive for accuracy, DiabetesGPT is designed as an educational tool and complement to professional medical care, not a replacement for it.

Our Values

Accessibility: We believe quality diabetes information should be available to everyone, regardless of location or resources.

Accuracy: We're committed to providing evidence-based information that aligns with current medical standards and best practices.

Empowerment: We aim to help people with diabetes gain confidence in making daily health decisions through better understanding.

Privacy: We respect user privacy and do not store personal health information shared during conversations with our AI.
